Things to Do in Santa Cruz!

In case we aren't keeping you busy enough during your stay in Santa Cruz, here is a list of some of our favorite fun and local things to do and experience!

Walking, Running, and Biking

  • West Cliff Drive: Exit out of the Dream Inn and Sea & Sand Inn and walk to your left (or right if you are at The Darling House) on the walking/bike path. Walk to the lighthouse and see the surfers and sea lions or walk even further (the whole trail is about 4 miles roundtrip).

  • Wilder Ranch State Park: Explore the preserved ranch buildings and seaside trail. Wilder Ranch is about a mile north of Santa Cruz on Highway 1.

  • Henry Cowell Redwoods State Park: Drive 20 minutes inland from Santa Cruz into the Santa Cruz Mountains and walk amongst the giant redwood trees!

  • Other Trails and Outdoors Adventures on Hilltromper.com

  • Bike Rentals nearby at Pacific Ave Cycles

Beaches and Tidepools

  • Natural Bridges State Beach and Tidepools: Our favorite tidepooling spot is at Natural Bridges. This beach and park is at the opposite end of West Cliff Drive from your hotel.

  • Cowells Beach: The closest beach to the hotels right in front of the Dream Inn.

  • Other Beaches on Hilltromper.com

Fun Places to Explore

  • Pacific Avenue in downtown Santa Cruz: It's a few minutes drive or a 20 minute walk from the hotel to meander on Pacific Avenue for shops, restaurants, sweets, and a lot of local Santa Cruz color (hippies!).

  • Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk: Ride the 5th oldest rollercoaster in the U.S.! Click here for September weekend hours

  • Movies downtown at the old-school movie theatre, The Del Mar
